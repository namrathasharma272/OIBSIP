About Dubai

Dubai is a city of skycrapers, ports, and beaches, where big business takes place alongside vibrant tourism. Dubai is relatively crime free place where administrative efficiency and openness to business have encouraged a outstanding growth.

Some Achievements

Burj Khalifa: Dubai's most iconic landmark, the Burj Khalifa, tallest building in the world and a global iconStanding at an impressive height of 828 meters, it was completed in 2010 by Emaar Properties.

Palm Jumeirah: It is a world-famous man-made island located in Dubai, designed in the shape of a palm tree. It is one of the largest artificial islands in the world and was developed by Nakheel. Known for its luxurious lifestyle, Palm Jumeirah is home to high-end hotels, upscale residences, restaurants, and entertainment venues, including the iconic Atlantis.

Dubai Metro: Dubai Metro is a modern and fully automated rail network in Dubai, known for its efficiency, cleanliness, and advanced technology. Launched in 2009, it was the first urban train system in the region and plays a vital role in the city public transportation.
